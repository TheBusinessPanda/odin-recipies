dosa

Ingredient -

  • daal
  • suji
  • coconut / peanuts
  • a selection of vegetables
  • potatoes
  • spices

Steps-

  1. mix the suji with water and let it ferment overnight
  2. prepare the potatoes and spices together to make a spicy potato mash
  3. spread the fermented batter on top of a large pan evenly in shape of a circle
  4. remove the dosa from the pan when golden brown
  5. prepare sambhar with veggies and a chutney with either peanuts of coconut
  6. put the potato stuffing inside the dosa and roll it up
  7. serve with sambhar and chutney and enjoy your meal
